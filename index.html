I work at the intersection of environmental science and institutional change — building the analytical frameworks, advisory approaches, and technical tools that help organizations move from sustainability intention to measurable practice.

My background spans field conservation in Kenya's arid lands, intergovernmental governance at the United Nations, climate finance training across East Africa, and co-founding a consultancy at the frontier of AI-powered ESG advisory. That breadth is not accidental — it is the foundation of how I think.

Based in Nairobi, working globally. Currently focused on Kenya's IFRS S1/S2 compliance landscape, Article 6.2 carbon market development, and building tools that make East African climate data accessible and actionable.
